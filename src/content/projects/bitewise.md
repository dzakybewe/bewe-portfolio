An Android app that helps users choose a restaurant based on their food preferences, built in an interdisciplinary team of Mobile, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing developers as part of Bangkit 2024 by Google, GoTo, Traveloka.

Everyone knows the feeling: it's lunchtime, there are too many options, and somehow no one can decide. BiteWise started from that exact frustration. As the mobile developer, I built the Android client in Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, integrating the recommendation engine our ML team built via an API from the Cloud team.

Being part of a team that spanned three disciplines, Mobile, ML, and Cloud, meant my work was only one piece of a larger system. The recommendation logic ended up simpler than expected, which taught me that the value of an application isn't always in technical complexity, sometimes it's in solving the problem well enough for the user.
