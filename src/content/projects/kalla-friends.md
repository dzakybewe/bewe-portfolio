Kalla Friends is the official loyalty app for Kalla Group, giving customers access to promos, vouchers, events, and product information across Kalla's businesses.

I joined the Kalla Friends team mid-project as part of my internship at DOT Indonesia. The app is offline-first, so one of my main contributions was migrating the local database from Hive to ObjectBox for better performance. I also implemented the vehicle service appointment flow, letting users book service appointments directly through the app.

Stepping into someone else's codebase and contributing without breaking things is a skill I hadn't fully appreciated before. I also learned the hard way that communication with mentors isn't optional. I wasn't proactive enough asking for feedback early on, and I learned from it. In a consultancy where every project is different, adapting fast and speaking up early makes all the difference.
